About BIMCOT

BIMCOT is a professional training organization that offers IRCA-certified training programs. We provide comprehensive training courses that prepare professionals to obtain IRCA certifications in various management system standards. While we deliver IRCA-certified training, BIMCOT is an independent training provider, not affiliated with IRCA.

About IRCA

The International Register of Certificated Auditors (IRCA) is the world's original and largest international certification body for auditors of management systems. BIMCOT offers training programs that prepare participants to obtain IRCA certifications, but BIMCOT is an independent training provider and is not affiliated with or endorsed by IRCA.
